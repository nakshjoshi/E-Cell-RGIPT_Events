EVENTS

E-Cell, RGIPT is a student-run body that fosters the spirit of entrepreneurship and innovation among students. Our main goal is to help and promote student entrepreneurs by giving them the tools, advice, and mentorship they need to launch their businesses. It also aids in the development of key abilities that are necessary for entrepreneurship, like creativity, problem-solving, leadership, and communication. The ultimate aim is to establish a thriving environment for innovation and entrepreneurship that benefits not only students but also the larger community.

Trade, Compete, Conquer: Traders Arena 2024

Traders Arena marked a historic milestone at RGIPT, hosting the first-ever Virtual Stock Market Simulation Competition in collaboration with Stockgro. It was an exciting chance for participants to begin their trading journey, channelling their inner Warren Buffet as they negotiated market dynamics and tested their financial skills. Armed with virtual funds totaling ₹10 Lacs, contestants devised strategies to weather the test of time, aiming to emerge as the ultimate profit-makers. The competition not only promised exciting cash prizes for the winners, but it also awarded each participant a Certificate of Participation to commemorate their foray into the world of financial markets.

E-Cell Orientation: Igniting Entrepreneurial Passion

The E-Cell Orientation event was a dynamic celebration of entrepreneurship, blending creativity with practicality. Attendees were welcomed into an atmosphere buzzing with innovative ideas and driven individuals. The E-Cell Orientation was more than just an event; it was a catalyst for future innovators and leaders in the world of business. A highlight of the event was the "Guess the Imposter" challenge, where participants, wearing headphones, danced to music while the audience tried to spot the one with different music playing. This interactive game added a fun and exciting element to the event, showcasing creativity and teamwork.

Talent Show: A Symphony of Diverse Talents

This event brought together a large number of people ready to express their unique abilities, resulting in an evening full of laughter, applause, and companionship. From captivating musical performances to thrilling dance routines, from side-splitting comedy sketches to profound spoken word recitations, the event showed the community's varied range of abilities and passions. Attendees were engrossed in the colourful atmosphere, cheering on acts and making new friends among the shared enthusiasm. It was held just after the start of the college term and provided students with a memorable opportunity to come together, appreciate their skills, and form long-lasting connections.
